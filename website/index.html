How it works

1
Turn Quassel on. A small pill sits at the bottom of your screen.
2
Click into any text field — editor, browser, chat, even a terminal.
3
Hold Ctrl+Meta and speak, then release — the text appears at your cursor. Or double-tap for hands-free.

Quassel is free and open source (MIT). Speech runs locally via whisper.cpp — nothing is uploaded.
